Major — Breaks a Brand Law

#2 — Terracotta Used as Card Background

Component: atelier-dashboard.tsx · Team Card

Problem

The Team card uses a full terracotta-tinted surface:

  • bg-[#fff8f5]
  • border-[#ffdbcf]/50
  • Terracotta avatars
  • #9a6f64 / #74584f text

Brand law: Terracotta is rationed to avatars + at most one badge per viewport; never a section background or full card.

The current viewport has 4+ terracotta instances: Step badge, Book-first-client icon tile, Team card background, and Team avatars.

Fix

  1. Make the Team card the same white sheet as the others:
    • Background: var(--al-surface-container-lowest)
    • Text: navy tokens
    • Icon tile: var(--al-surface-container)
  2. Keep the small terracotta avatar cluster — that's a legitimate avatar use.
  3. Drop terracotta from one of the two badges so only one terracotta badge remains per viewport.
