## Context (Minto Pyramid Principle)

- **Situation** — The team needs to reliably obtain product collection and delivery information.
- **Complication** — There's uncertainty about the exact source of this data and whether existing endpoints can be repurposed for the basket page.
- **Key Questions** — Can the PDP endpoint be used on the basket page? Who can provide definitive information on collection data?
- **Objective** — To identify the authoritative sources for product collection and delivery data and confirm their usability for the current project.

## Notes

- A key point of uncertainty is whether the PDP team will allow their endpoint to be used directly on the basket page.
- There's a suggestion to ask "credit rating investigators" (likely a transcription error for a data or analytics team) about how to obtain collection information.
