# Project Priorities Questionnaire

To help clarify the project's strategic direction, please answer the following questions:

1.  What is the primary business objective for introducing promo codes in Q4? (e.g., Increase conversion, acquire new customers, drive sales of specific items, match a competitor feature, etc.)

2.  What is the most important aspect of the user experience for this first release? Please rank the following from 1 (most important) to 4 (least important):
    *   A seamless, uninterrupted journey (no need to re-enter codes).
    *   Clarity and prevention of any possible confusion.
    *   Consistency between the web and app experience.
    *   Speed of the basket and checkout process.

3.  What is the acceptable level of technical complexity and risk for the initial Q4 launch? Are you prioritizing a quick, simple launch to meet the deadline, or is it acceptable to take on more complexity for a more robust feature?

4.  From a business perspective, what is the bigger risk we should prioritize avoiding?
    *   A) The risk of customer confusion/frustration (e.g., a promo code disappearing after they change their basket).
    *   B) The risk of engineering cost and complexity delaying the launch or introducing bugs.

5.  Is there a plan or desire for a more advanced, state-synced promotion system in the future, or is the simple, non-persistent solution likely to be sufficient for the long term?
