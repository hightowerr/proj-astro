# Problem Diagnosis: Argos Pay Customer Journey Strategy

The core problem is to determine the optimal strategy for integrating "Argos Pay," a new strategic credit proposition, into the customer journey. 

The key challenge is to balance two competing goals:
1.  **Driving Business Growth:** The feature is intended to be a "force multiplier" that increases sales, revenue, and average basket size.
2.  **Building Customer Trust:** The integration must be "seamless" and not aggressive ("thrown in their faces"), as customers are inherently cautious about credit and desire trust and transparency above all.

The analysis must therefore focus on how to introduce this new affordability option in a way that feels helpful and trustworthy to the customer, rather than intrusive, thereby achieving the desired business outcomes.
