**2. Ranking Criteria**

This is the most crucial part. For your top-ranked shows (e.g., your top 5), could you explain *why* you ranked them so highly? What specific attributes or qualities make them stand out to you?

Consider aspects like:
*   **Genre:** (e.g., Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Crime, Comedy, Drama)
*   **Tone:** (e.g., Dark, Humorous, Serious, Satirical, Uplifting)
*   **Plot:** (e.g., Complex and multi-layered, simple and character-driven, fast-paced, slow-burn)
*   **Characters:** (e.g., Deep character development, strong ensemble cast, compelling anti-hero)
*   **Themes:** (e.g., Philosophical questions, political commentary, family dynamics)
*   **Style:** (e.g., Visually stunning cinematography, unique narrative structure)

*(Example for "The Sopranos")*
