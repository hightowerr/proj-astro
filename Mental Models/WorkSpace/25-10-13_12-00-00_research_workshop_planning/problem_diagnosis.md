# Problem Diagnosis: Planning the Research Question–Gathering Workshop

The primary challenge is to design and execute a highly effective workshop to define the critical research questions for the Basket Redesign project. The workshop must be conducted before the peak retail season begins.

**Key success criteria for the workshop include:**

- **Clarity:** The output should be a clear, prioritized list of research questions.
- **Alignment:** All key stakeholders (Product, Engineering, Design, Data) should be aligned on these questions.
- **Actionability:** The questions should be framed in a way that they can be realistically answered through user research.
- **Efficiency:** The workshop itself must be a productive use of everyone's time, especially given the pre-peak time constraints.
