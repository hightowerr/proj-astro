The user wants to validate and refresh customer evidence related to known problems with a shopping basket/cart. The ultimate goal is to inform a redesign of the basket that solves real user problems, which is expected to improve the conversion rate.

The task originates from a "Basket Redesign Research Kickoff" meeting, which implies that some "existing problems" and "customer evidence" may already be documented within the project. 

The core of the task is to apply a structured thinking process to ensure this foundational research is robust, complete, and free from bias before proceeding with the redesign.
