## Mental Model = Promethean Technology

**Category = Science / Systems Thinking**
**Description:**
Promethean technology is a typology of technology that can drive explosive societal expansion because it is *viable* in an energetic sense: after being set up by the currently available technology, it can reproduce itself while generating a surplus of energy. This surplus enables autocatalytic scaling and can intensify Jevons/Malthusian dynamics.

**When to Avoid (or Use with Caution):**
- When the technology is only feasible but not viable (depends on external energy subsidies).
- When you’re using “Promethean” as hype rather than an energy-balance criterion.

**Keywords for Situations:**
Energy transitions; breakthrough tech; viability; surplus; autocatalysis; overshoot.

**Thinking Steps:**
1. Separate feasibility (can it work?) from viability (can it energetically reproduce itself?).
2. Evaluate life-cycle energy inputs/outputs and reproduction requirements.
3. Identify the positive feedback loops enabled by surplus energy.
4. Stress-test environmental and resource constraints under rapid scaling.
5. Consider governance to prevent runaway overshoot.

**Coaching Questions:**
- Can this technology reproduce itself with surplus energy, or does it require ongoing subsidies?
- What “special fuel” or constraint does it unlock?
- What runaway scaling loop does it enable?
